What is VFX?


VFX (Visual Effects) is the process of creating or enhancing scenes using digital technology that cannot be filmed in real life.

History of VFX


VFX started with simple effects in early cinema and evolved with computers to create modern CGI movies.

How VFX Works


  • Planning & Storyboarding
  • Green Screen Shooting
  • 3D Modeling & Animation
  • Compositing
  • Final Rendering

Types of VFX


CGI (Computer Generated Imagery)

Creating digital characters, objects and environments.

Compositing

Combining multiple layers into one realistic scene.

Motion Capture

Recording human movements for digital characters.

Green Screen Effects

Replacing background digitally.

Matte Painting

Creating digital landscapes and backgrounds.

Simulation Effects

Fire, smoke, water, explosions etc.

Tools & Software


After Effects

Motion graphics & effects.

Nuke

3D effects & animation.

  • Heavy computer systems needed
