What is Stop Motion Animation?


Stop motion is an animation technique where physical objects are moved slightly and photographed frame by frame to create motion.

History of Stop Motion


Stop motion began in the late 19th century and became popular in films like King Kong (1933). and modern movies such as Coraline.

How Stop Motion Animation Works


  • Step 1: Create characters & objects
  • Step 2: Set up camera and lighting
  • Step 3: Move objects slightly
  • Step 4: Capture each frame
  • Step 5: Combine frames into video

Advantages of Stop Motion Animation


Realistic Physical Movements

Highly Creative & Artistic Style

Natural Textures & Lighting

Can Be Made With Simple Tools

Disadvantages of Stop Motion Animation


  • Very time-consuming animation process
  • Requires high patience and accuracy
  • Small mistakes can ruin scenes
  • Needs stable camera & lighting setup
