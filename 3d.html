What is 3D Animation?

Basic understanding of three-dimensional animation


3D Animation is a digital animation technique where objects and characters are created in three dimensions (length, width, depth). It gives realistic depth, lighting, and camera movement, making animations look close to real life.

History of 3D Animation


3D animation developed with computer graphics in the late 20th century. The first fully 3D animated movie was Toy Story (1995). Since then, 3D animation has become the backbone of movies, games, advertisements, and simulations.
