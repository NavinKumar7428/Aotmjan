What is 2D Animation?

Basic understanding of two-dimensional animation

2D Animation is a technique where characters, objects, and backgrounds are created in a flat, two-dimensional space (height and width). Movement is shown by displaying a sequence of drawings or images one after another.

History of 2D Animation

2D animation started in the early 1900s with hand-drawn frames. Disney and Warner Bros popularized it in cartoons. With computers, digital 2D animation became faster and more efficient.

Disadvantages of 2D Animation

  • Limited realism / depth
  • Time-consuming for complex scenes
  • Requires strong drawing skills
